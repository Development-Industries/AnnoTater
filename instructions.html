How to Use AnnoTater

This extension allows you to highlight text, add comments, and take screenshots effectively.

  • To highlight text, select the text and click the 'Highlight' button.
  • To add a comment, select text and click the 'Comment' button.
  • To remove a highlight, click the 'Remove Highlight' button.
  • To take a screenshot, click the 'Screenshot' button.
